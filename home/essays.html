The Dull Mumbling

May 11, 2021

Is the power of words meaningful to everybody. I give it a doubt.
The world is full of complex yet contradictory emergence in this twenty
first cencury. Yet eveerything refreshed but rotten to their own roots,
with a few see and conceive.
Can this world, however, give it's own miricles to you, my friends?
Will it show its own will, as morning stars arise in the glory dawn,
the moisture, dews and birds singing like wild carnivals.
O Holy Father, our mysteries be within our own body, the fresh air truly
leading to the wonderland of youth.
